EOLANG Maven Plugin

Put your sources into src/main/eo and then add this to your pom.xml:

<project>
  <build>
    <plugins>
      <plugin>
        <groupId>org.eolang</groupId>
        <artifactId>eo-maven-plugin</artifactId>
        <version>0.59.3</version>
        <executions>
          <execution>
            <goals>
              <goal>register</goal>
              <goal>assemble</goal>
              <goal>transpile</goal>
              <goal>copy</goal>
              <goal>unplace</goal>
              <goal>unspile</goal>
            </goals>
          </execution>
        </executions>
      </plugin>
    </plugins>
    [...]
  </build>
  [...]
</project>

More information you can find at www.eolang.org.
